If you made an incredibly important discovery, how would you share it?

Nothing is more important than an accurate, biblical understanding of salvation. Jesus promised that many would come to him on the day of judgment thinking they would be accepted into His eternal blessed presence, only to be turned away and rejected.

We are a ministry of Christ Covenant Church in Fall River, Massachusetts. We have a deep concern for the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ, as it has been revealed by the Scriptures. Our belief is that many believe a partial gospel, taught from the traditions of the Protestant Reformation, not from the truth of the Bible. Our hope is that this website will challenge you to think more deeply and more biblically about the gospel message "once for all passed down to the saints."
